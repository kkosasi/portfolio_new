01

career.



How did I get into design?


I've always been an artist, but the concept of digital design didn’t come into my life until high school. In time of curiosity, I joined the Graphic Design class to fill in my empty schedule. Still didn’t understood it in the beginning, but I fell in love with it in college where I decided to pursue it full time. Design is a way for me to communicate clearly through art. It is not ambiguous and left to interpretation. It is a way to touch emotions, and waver hearts through visual communication. The creativity of marketing that is involved in design drives my passion. I strive to keep clarity of communications in my designs.


What kind of work environment do I thrive in and where do I see myself in 5 years?


A place where I can be myself, exercise and grow my design skills. An upbeat, friendly, and breathable work culture is important for me. Having someone to learn from is also a great plus! In the next 5 years I would love to learn 3D modeling, and doing UI/UX full time in a non-office environment. I would love to continue doing remote work due to it being environmentally friendly, and it saves time from commuting. During the pandemic I realized that the set up I have at home is a lot faster, creating a more efficient work progress. Mentally, I had actual spare time to take extra classes, care for my dogs, hike, go to the doctor, and have time for a healthier lifestyle due to being in the comfort of my home. I want to use my creativity thoroughly in an active work environment. Being the jack of all trades is a my end goal as a designer. I would love to be able to be the person who can do-it-all.


What am I currently doing to improve my skills?


I am currently enrolled in a UI/UX bootcamp program in order to boost my career, skills, experience and knowledge in the field. I am hoping to go into this route in the design world in the future.

personal.



Born in Indonesia, I moved to Atlanta when I was 15 and have built my life here ever since.


My love for adventure:


Travelling is one of my biggest passion in life and I'd like to continue doing so before it drags me to settle down. I don’t plan on stopping because these explorations and adventures have inspired, educated, and pushed me in ways that I can’t begin to imagine. It has affected my views of their society, culture, food and lives, and also, my design inspirations. Exploration and adventure of experiencing other cultures are exciting!


Other Interests:


Other hobbies I fancy includes hiking, 3D prop building for costumes, illustration, vlogging, photography, gaming and trying out new foods! These are some of other artistic hobbies I took up along the way to get to where I am today. Check out the tab under about me to peek at some of the works from my hobbies!
